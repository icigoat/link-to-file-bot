⚙️ Setup Required

Please set the CHANNEL_ID environment variable in your Render dashboard.

Steps:

  1. Go to your Render dashboard
  2. Select your service
  3. Go to "Environment" tab
  4. Add: CHANNEL_ID = @yourchannel or -1001234567890
  5. Save and redeploy
Note: Use your channel username (e.g., @mychannel) or numeric ID (e.g., -1001234567890)
